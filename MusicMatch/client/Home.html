Music Recommendation

This application intends to create a platform that someone can use to discover both people AND music. The app analyzes user's music preferences so new songs are recommended based on whether they like a song or not. Many music apps nowadays have shortcomings in the way users find music, so our app is a more interactive and unique method that music listeners can use.

Getting Started

To begin your music discovery journey, click on the sign in button on the top right of this page. Once you enter the details of your Spotify account, you will be taken back to this page. You can then click on the discover page to start finding the music you love!
